Privacy Policy Last updated: January 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Rumker does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. Security and Privacy are fundamental commitments that guide everything we do as a cyber intelligence platform.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

In line with our commitment to privacy and security, Rumker explicitly does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (names, addresses, phone numbers)
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Browsing history or website usage data
  • Device information or IP addresses
  • Location data or geolocation information
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Any form of user analytics or behavioral data
  • Financial information or payment details

2. Our Privacy-First Approach

As a cyber intelligence and security platform, we understand the critical importance of privacy. Our approach includes:

No Data Collection

We have designed our platform to operate without collecting any user data, ensuring complete privacy protection.

Security by Design

Privacy and security are built into every aspect of our platform architecture and development process.

Transparency

We maintain complete transparency about our data practices and privacy commitments.

Compliance

We adhere to the highest privacy standards and regulatory requirements in all jurisdictions.

3. Website Usage

When you visit our website:

  • No personal information is collected or stored
  • No tracking cookies are placed on your device
  • No analytics or monitoring systems track your visit
  • Your IP address is not logged or stored
  • No third-party tracking or advertising systems are used

4. Contact Forms

If you choose to contact us through our website forms:

  • Information provided is used solely for responding to your inquiry
  • No data is stored beyond what is necessary for communication
  • Information is not shared with third parties
  • You can request deletion of any correspondence at any time

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services for functionality, but these are selected for their privacy-first approach:

  • CDN services for content delivery (no user tracking)
  • Font services that do not collect personal data
  • No advertising networks or tracking services
  • No social media tracking or integration

6. Data Security

While we don't collect user data, we maintain the highest security standards:

  • Enterprise-grade security infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and monitoring systems
  • Compliance with industry security standards

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to:

  • Access or request copies of
  • Correct or update
  • Delete or request deletion of
  • Port to another service
  • Object to processing of

However, if you have any privacy concerns or questions, we are committed to addressing them promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our website contact form.

Our Commitment to You

At Rumker, we believe that privacy is not just a legal requirement but a fundamental right. As a cyber intelligence platform, we understand the importance of protecting privacy and maintaining trust. Our commitment to not collecting user data reflects our core values and our dedication to the highest standards of security and privacy.
